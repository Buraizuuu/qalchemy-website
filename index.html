Why QAlchemy

Manual test writing doesn't scale with how fast requirements change.

Traditional Test Case Writing

  • Time: Hours per feature, manually drafted
  • Coverage: Depends on individual thoroughness
  • Edge Cases: Easy to overlook under deadline pressure
  • Consistency: Varies tester to tester
  • Traceability: Maintained by hand, often stale
  • Productivity: Test writing competes with test execution time

QAlchemy AI Powered

  • Time: Comprehensive test cases in a single AI call
  • Coverage: 11 categories generated systematically
  • Edge Cases: Boundary & risk based scenarios by design
  • Consistency: Same senior QA reasoning, every time
  • Traceability: Matrix generated automatically from criteria
  • Productivity: Review & edit ready output, export anywhere
